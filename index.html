HOW TO PLAY
  1. 01
    Start the test

    Click INITIALIZE TEST or press SPACE. The 3D scene will begin calibrating.

  2. 02
    Wait for the signal

    The screen will show a pulsing amber indicator. The GO signal appears after a random 1–5 second delay — you can't predict it.

  3. 03
    React instantly

    When the green GO! signal appears, click the button or press SPACE as fast as possible. Every millisecond counts.

  4. 04
    Don't jump the gun

    Clicking before the signal counts as a false start. The test resets and you'll need to try again.

  5. 05
    Track your progress

    Your best time, session average, and full history are tracked automatically. Run multiple attempts to see improvement.

TIPS FOR A BETTER SCORE
  • ◈ Keep your finger hovering over the button — don't lift it between runs
  • ◈ Focus on the 3D scene, not the button — the color shift is your earliest cue
  • ◈ Stay relaxed — tension slows muscle response time
  • ◈ Use SPACE instead of clicking for a slightly faster response
  • ◈ Warm up with 3–5 runs before attempting a personal best
